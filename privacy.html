Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Ronanto Interactive operates the website located at ronanto.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit this website and how it is handled. This site is a static informational website — there are no user accounts, no contact forms, and no advertising.

Information We Collect

This website does not directly collect personal information. There are no sign-up forms, comment sections, or login systems.

However, like most websites, visiting ronanto.com may result in your web browser sending standard technical data to the hosting server. This can include:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • The page(s) you visited and the time of the visit
  • The referring website (if you followed a link here)
  • Your device's operating system

This data is typically recorded in server logs, retained for a limited period, and used solely for operational purposes such as diagnosing technical issues. It is not used to build profiles or track individual visitors.

Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics trackers, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking scripts. No data about your browsing behavior is gathered, stored, or shared.

External Links

This website may contain links to external websites — for example, social media platforms. Once you leave ronanto.com, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. Those external sites have their own privacy practices, and Ronanto Interactive has no control over or responsibility for them. Review their policies before providing any personal information.

Google Fonts

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts. When your browser requests these fonts, Google may receive your IP address and browser information. Google's own Privacy Policy governs how that data is handled.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13, and no information is knowingly collected from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
